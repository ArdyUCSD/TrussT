Welcome!

This website is to be used as a resource for the Port Authority to check the progress of Truss-T Engineering's crane project, so there are no miscommunications and any questions/concerns can be addressed immediately. Our novel crane design aims to provide the San Diego Port Authority with a cost-effective crane design that can accurately place cargo onto a ship in the shortest amount of time allowable. Truss-T Engineering has designed an extremely efficient crane, which utilizes its mechanical system seamlessly with the overall structure. By clicking on the Design section, one can see our most current design and analysis. Also, the Financial section of this site will provide one with multiple interactive graphs that show statistics such as the percentage of overall cost for each component that goes into building the system.

In addition, Truss-T Engineering wishes to be as transparent as possible for the client's benefit. By showing the financial information and justification behind important design changes over the entirety of the project, a trust can be developed that can lead to future business between Truss-T Engineering and the Port Authority.
